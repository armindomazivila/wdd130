Maputo, Moz

Maputo is Mozambique's capital city. Before independence, it was called Lourenço Marques. It is located on the southeast coast of Africa along the Indian Ocean. The city is rich in culture and gastronomy. Most of the population practices Christianity, while others follow Islam and traditional beliefs. Maputo is popular for seafood, beaches, ocean views, and its warm tropical climate.
